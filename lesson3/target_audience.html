Target Audience

This page is aimed mainly at local merchants in the Valle Grande community, Lampa commune in Santiago de Chile.Valle Grande is a growing community that currently has about 18,000 inhabitants. These site focus on people over 16 years of age, who have personal or family businesses, many of them in their own houses, and who have difficulty promoting and making themselves known within the community. At the same time, it is aimed at the Valle Grande community in general, since all people can visit it as clients while they seek to cover any need through the providers promoted by the site.
