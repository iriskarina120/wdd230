Site Name

Publica-T Chamber of Commerce of Valle Grande

Site Purpose

This site was designed for the community of Valle Grande in general but, particularly for those entrepreneurs who are working for their businesses to achieve a greater reach in the community. Maybe their potential customers do not know that what they are looking for is around the corner! What we do in “Publica-T” is provide entrepreneurs with an attractive and easy-to-use platform to present their company and be part of a supplier catalog that will show the clients who has what they are looking for within their communities and how to contact the supplier, benefiting each other, entrepreneurs and community. Also, it will keep the community informed about local events, announcements and news.
