Repetition

BYU Pathway Worldwide
byupathway.org

This site is a clear example of the repetition principle. A great harmony is appreciated in the design, due to the fact that the style of the fonts, colors, navigation structure, elements, content, among others, is maintained. This gives a great sense of cohesion to the site.

Contrast

Disney Plus
disneyplus.com

The Disney Plus website has a wonderful contrast. It really is very attractive on a visual level, since it presents a background that improves the visibility of the content and makes it easier to read and appreciate. The images are and the content is both very well positioned..

White Space

familysearch
familysearch.org

Familysearch is a site that represents the principle of white Space very well. Design elements are placed in a way that favors readability and visual harmony. There are adequate paddings, use of blank spaces making the content easily understandable.
